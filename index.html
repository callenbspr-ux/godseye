GODSEYE — How It Works
A market intelligence terminal that watches the world so you don't have to.
The Workflow Pipeline
Every 30 minutes during market hours, GODSEYE runs automatically through this sequence:
📰
Step 1 — News Scan
Pulls headlines from Reuters, AP, BBC, CNBC, MarketWatch + 5 others. Classifies each as Bullish/Bearish/Neutral. Tags which instruments each headline affects.
↓
📈
Step 2 — Live Prices
Fetches real-time prices for XAUUSD, WTI, S&P 500, NASDAQ, BTC, ETH (tradable) + DXY, VIX, 10Y, DOW (barometers — context only, not traded).
↓
🔮
Step 3 — Polymarket Scan
Searches active prediction markets for geopolitics, Fed policy, commodities and crypto. Shows top bets by volume with direct links.
↓
🐟
Step 4 — MiroFish Comparison
MiroFish base probabilities (from swarm simulations) are compared to Polymarket live odds. Large gap = potential mispricing = high-confidence signal.
↓
⚡
Step 5 — Trading Signal Generated
Composite score = News (30%) + Polymarket/MiroFish divergence (30%) + Momentum (20%) + DXY correlation (10%) + VIX (10%). Outputs BUY / SELL / NEUTRAL with entry price, expected % move, and confidence.
Reading the Dashboard

Signal Confidence Guide
BUY Composite score > +0.15. Bullish confluence across sources.
SELL Composite score < -0.15. Bearish confluence.
NEUTRAL Mixed signals. No clear edge.
HIGH — Score >0.40, multiple confirming factors
MEDIUM — Score 0.20–0.40
LOW — Score <0.20, weak signal

MiroFish Integration
MiroFish base probabilities are currently stored in the pipeline (Gold=81%, BTC=83% bullish etc.) and updated manually after each sim run.
To update: Run MiroFish sim in Cowork → note bullish % per instrument → update MIROFISH_BULLISH in fetch_live_data.py → push to GitHub.
